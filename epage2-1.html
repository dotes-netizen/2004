The materials of making flour miniatures are simple. We can make it by the common foodstuffs at home.
 
Flour
Flour, which has many usages in daily life, is the main material in making flour miniatures. The reason is that it is not too hard or soft. 		  Glutinous rice powder
It can increase the toughness of the dough because of its super stickiness. That can help the dough easy to pinch.
   
Salt
Its function is to keep the dough from hardening. 		   
The materials below can be bought in chemical or food products stores. Paying attention that the flour miniature can't be eaten if you add the materials below.
 
Alum
Its function is against the humidity and insects. 		  Antiseptic
Its function is to prevent the dough from mildewing. Remember to use the hydrophilic Antiseptic.
   
Pigment
Let skin-white dough become other colors. We can use chemical pigments or eating pigments. Only the flour miniature added the eating pigments can be eaten.
