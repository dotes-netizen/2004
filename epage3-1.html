Demo 1 ( A Cute Doll )
=>

=>


1. Rub yellow and round dough with two hands.

2. And then rub into an oval.

3. Use point finger and thumb to rub into a shape like a water drop.



=>
=>

4. Stick eyes. Use white dough to be the white of eyes.

5. Add eyebrows

6. Use the pen point to press the mouth.

=>
=>

7. Stick flat pink dough to the cheek.

8. Use long and thin dough to be legs and make feet.

9. Stick long and thin arms.

=>
=>

10. Pinch a small water drop shape.

11. Press an indentation line by a tool.

12. Flatten it to be hands.

=>
=>

13. Stick hands.

14. The back of sticking hands.

15. Rub long dough to be hair.



=>
=>

16. Make three different sizes of heart shapes to be hair.

17. Finish.



18. Look from the top.




19. It's more beautiful to spray the light and limpid paint.
