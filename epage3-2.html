Demo 2 ( Micky Mouse )

=> 
=> 

1. Rub lone shape dough. Insert a bamboo stick in the midpoint and curve down.



2. Flatten oval shape dough around the former one to be pants.

3. Make circle shape to be shoes.
=>
=>

4. Finish shoes.

5. Flatten oval shape dough around the former one to be clothes.

6. And make arms.
=>
=>

7. Make circle shape to be gloves.

8. Stick round dough on the top to be head.

9. Stick two small circles to ears. Stick big and small circle dough to be face.
=>
=>

10. Add eyes. Make a water drop shape to be nose.

11. Pick a mouth by tool.

12. Finish.
