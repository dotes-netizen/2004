Demo 3 ( The Dianosorse )

 
 =>

1. Rub a water drop shape.

2. The finger press one side and move around.
3. Finish the head.

 

 
=>

4. Use the tool to pick eyes and put white dough into them. And stick black dough.

5. Rub four long shapes to be legs and feet.
6. Stick small points to be toes.

 

 
=>

7. Use different colors to rub water drop shapes to be the bones of stegosaur.

8. Flatten them.
9. Finish.

 

 
=>

10. It's more beautiful to spray the light and limpid paint.
