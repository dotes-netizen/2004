The purpose of the flour miniature in Taiwan is for making children toys. People can see it in a temple fair or a garden fair. Generally speaking, we can divide the flour miniature by manufacture process into two sects. One is color-mixing flour miniature, the other is color-smearing flour miniature.

Color-mixing flour miniature
The dough must be added different colors at first. And then using the different color dough to pinch things. Shih Chin-Chiang, who lives in Lu-Gang in Taiwan, is the master worker of the sect. He is over 70 years old. In the beginning, he learned this craft just for his interest. He has devoted himself to this job for more than 50 years. Now he has many children and grandchildren, he need not worry about the life. He taught people to make the flour miniature in his free time. He hopes that the traditional craft can be passed to next generation.

Color-smearing flour miniature
The flour must be added cold water to stir and become like paste. And steam it in a pot. After steaming, rub it to mix well. At the same time, add some alum powder and terra alba. Then pinch the shapes you want and dry it in the shade. Finally, paint the bottom first. Second, paint the head. And then paint the body. Hsu An-Tien is good at the color-smearing flour miniature. This kind of flour miniature is the sacrifices. There are 24 different things-12 things for delicacies from the mountains and 12 things for choice seafood. That is because the transportation was not convenient before. People who lived in the mountains were not easy to get delicacies from the mountains. And people who lived near the sea were not easy to get choice seafood. They all used the flour miniature to substitute. In the course of time, the flour miniature has been an important sacrifice.

Shih Chiao-Yung
He is the master worker in Lu-Gang in Taiwan. He has written two books about the flour miniature. When he was 7 years old, he followed his family to make a living as a traditional craft maker. He has been to Paris to show the craft. He has deep feeling and enthusiasm for this craft.

Shih Jui-Lin
He lives in Bai-Gang in Taiwan. There are a lot of temple ceremonies there. He has engaged in the flour miniature for more than sixty years. However, the craft has been on the downgrade by the development of civilization. In Bai-Gang, Shih Jui-Lin is the only person to pass the craft.

Huang Ching-Nan
He was born in 1915 in Chang-Hua in Taiwan. He is famous by making the flower of flour miniature. He is also the first man who got the reward of handing down the traditional crafts in Taiwan. He is the person of enhance the flour miniature. He uses the commercial pigments to paint the dough and spray the clear lacquer. The work he made can reflect the limpid and fresh luster. In fact, he combines the flour miniature and the colored drawing.

Liu Chia-Tsai
He is a master worker of the young generation. Glass craft is his original job. He adds quarts, silicon, and chemical materials into the ingredients. After pinching, put the work into boiled water. This makes the work have the maximum elasticity just like rubber. He calls it "Rubber Model" and applies for a patent in Taiwan. It is said that "Rubber Model" can be kept for a hundred years.
